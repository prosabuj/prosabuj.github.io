Turning ideas into real products

I’m a self-taught Full-Stack JavaScript developer from Bangladesh. Instead of the traditional academic path, I dedicated myself completely to software — and what started as curiosity became my career.

Today, as Co-Founder of Humance AI Labs, I work across the stack — from polished frontends to robust APIs and well-structured data — helping our team build AI-powered products end to end. I care about simple, scalable, maintainable software that solves real problems.

Much of my work centers on AI and ML — a field I work in daily and never stop learning about. With our team, we build agentic, multimodal systems that generate video, image, audio, and music, orchestrating 150+ AI models into products people genuinely use. I love turning cutting-edge AI into smarter, more capable tools.
